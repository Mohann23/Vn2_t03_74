What is big data?
================
Big data are extremely large data sets that may be analyzed computationally to reveal patterns, trends and associations, especially relating to human behavior and interactions.



5 V's of Big Data:
=================
Velocity 
Volume
Variety
Veracity
Value


What is Hadoop?
>Hadoop is a framework which is used to store Big Data Across a spectrum of devices.
>This is done to help you process Big Data in Parallel

Three Important components in the Hadoop Ecosystem that you should know:
==========
1.Hdfs  (Parallel Storage Layer)
2.MapReduce   (Parallel Processing Layer)
3.YARN   (Resouce negotiator)
